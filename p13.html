Defination of Perplexity

Perplexity is an AI-powered search engine and conversational chatbot that uses large language models (LLMs) together with real-time web search to provide accurate, detailed, and up-to-date answers. Unlike traditional search engines that mainly display a list of links, Perplexity generates direct answers in natural language and includes citations to the sources used, allowing users to verify the information.
