Defination of Claude AI

Claude is a family of conversational AI assistants and advanced large language models (LLMs) developed by Anthropic. It is designed to understand natural language, write, code, analyze data, and solve complex problems. Claude is widely recognized for its strong reasoning capabilities, nuanced writing, and focus on safe, reliable outputs.
  • How Claude Works

    • Constitutional AI: Anthropic trains Claude using a set of guiding ethical principles (its "constitution") to ensure the AI remains helpful, honest, and harmless.

    Advanced Context: Claude is built to digest and analyze massive amounts of text at once, making it highly effective for parsing long reports, coding entire projects, or summarizing documents.

    Multimodal and Tool Use: Beyond text, Claude can understand documents, browse the web, and even execute "computer use" tasks by simulating keyboard and mouse input.
