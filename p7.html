Defination of Gen AI

Generative AI (GenAI) is a branch of artificial intelligence that creates new, original content—such as text, images, audio, and code—by learning patterns from existing training data. Unlike traditional AI that just classifies or analyzes existing data, GenAI simulates human creativity to generate novel outputs.

How it Works


GenAI relies heavily on deep learning models and neural networks (such as Transformers, GANs, and VAEs). During the training process, these models ingest vast datasets of text, images, or code. By analyzing the statistical relationships between different elements, they learn to predict what should come next in a sequence or how to properly structure a visual.
