Defination of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, decision-making, and problem-solving. To understand how it functions and impacts the digital landscape, it helps to break it down into a few core areas:How AI WorksUnlike traditional software that relies strictly on predefined rules and human commands, AI systems are built on data, algorithms, and computational power. By processing vast amounts of data, AI models recognize patterns and continuously adjust their settings to improve accuracy and make autonomous decisions.

Key Technologies

Machine Learning (ML): A subset of AI that enables computers to learn from historical data and make predictions without being explicitly programmed.

Deep Learning: A more advanced subset of ML that uses multilayered "neural networks" (modeled after the human brain) to extract features from unstructured data.

Natural Language Processing (NLP): Technology that allows computers to understand, interpret, and generate human languages.
