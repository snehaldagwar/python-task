Defination of Agentic AI

Agentic AI refers to autonomous artificial intelligence systems that set their own goals, break them into logical steps, and execute tasks without needing step-by-step human instructions. Unlike traditional chatbots that merely react to prompts, agentic AI proactively plans, uses digital tools, and learns from its environment to achieve complex objectives. Key CharacteristicsAutonomy & Proactivity: Agentic systems don't just answer questions; they turn knowledge into action. They can analyze a problem, formulate a multi-step plan, and execute it independently.Tool Usage: They are equipped with capabilities to interact with external software—such as APIs, databases, or search engines—to accomplish their goals.Adaptability: If they encounter obstacles or make mistakes, they are designed to self-correct, diagnose the issue, and refine their approach.
