Defination of Google Gemini

Google Gemini is a family of advanced, multimodal artificial intelligence models and assistant platforms developed by Google. Designed from the ground up to process multiple types of information simultaneously, it can seamlessly understand and generate text, audio, images, video, and computer programming code.Gemini functions as a general-purpose AI model as well as a consumer-facing chatbot. It powers various Google services, serving as a productivity and research assistant.
