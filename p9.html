Defination of ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a conversational artificial intelligence chatbot developed by OpenAI. It uses massive, deep-learning language models to understand natural human language and generate human-like text, audio, and images in response to user prompts.
  • Does the Acronym Stand For?

    • Chat: Communicating with a computer program in a natural, conversational, back-and-forth dialogue format.

    G – Generative: The AI is designed to create new content (such as text, essays, code, or images) rather than simply analyzing or repeating existing data.

    P – Pre-trained: The model was trained on billions of pages of data from books, websites, and articles before being fine-tuned by humans.

    T – Transformer: A specialized deep-learning neural network architecture that parses language, understands context, and predicts the most statistically probable next words.
